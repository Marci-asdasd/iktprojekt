Our Products

City cars

Standard American police lightbars are typically mounted on top of law enforcement vehicles and feature a combination of flashing red, blue, and sometimes white lights. These lightbars are designed for high visibility, ensuring that emergency vehicles are easily identifiable in various conditions, from day to night. They often include multiple strobe or rotating lights, providing a rapid sequence of flashes to alert other drivers and pedestrians of the vehicle's presence. Lightbars are crucial tools for maintaining public safety, directing traffic, and signaling emergencies.

High speed

High-speed American police lightbars are specifically designed for pursuit vehicles and high-performance law enforcement operations. These lightbars feature advanced LED technology, offering intense brightness and faster response times for improved visibility during high-speed chases. Their streamlined design minimizes wind resistance while maximizing the effectiveness of emergency lighting. Often incorporating specialized patterns like alternating strobe or directional signals, these lightbars enhance both the safety and effectiveness of law enforcement during high-intensity situations.

Off-road

Off-road American police lightbars are specially designed for law enforcement vehicles that operate in rugged, remote, or challenging environments. These lightbars are built to withstand harsh conditions, featuring durable, shock-resistant housings and powerful LED lights for optimal visibility in low-light or off-road scenarios. They often include flood and spot lighting capabilities to illuminate wide areas or focus on specific targets, aiding in search-and-rescue or tactical operations. With their tough construction and versatile lighting patterns, off-road lightbars help ensure police vehicles remain effective in all terrains.
